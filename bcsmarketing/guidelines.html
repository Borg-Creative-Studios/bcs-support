    BCS Marketing Guidelines

    Safety

    As a family-friendly organization, we prohibit any ads that are not
    rated for general audiences. This includes the ad's content, product,
    and link. We do not allow offensive ads, ads that misinform, ads for
    products or services that are not family-friendly, ads that promote
    illegal behavior, or ads promoting illegal product.

    Ads that promote, encourage, or represent violent or illegal content in any way, shape, or form will not be permitted.

    Ads cannot advertise NSFW, R-rated, or X-rated elements or topics.

    Media

    Ad media and/or image cannot contain NSFW, R-rated, or X-rated elements or topics.

    Links

    Links to your website or product are NOT to be shortened links. This is to insure security, privacy, and quality.

    Privacy

    Your personal information and contact information will not be shared
    unless required by law. Your corporate/organization/business name may be public through ad identifiers. Your payment may be processed through Wave Finance or PayPal. Your payment information will not be shared
    outside of our payment partners unless required by law.
