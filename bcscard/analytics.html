    "When will my card's analytics be available?"

    Updated analytics for cards become available every Monday.

    "Where can I find my analytics?"

    All BCS Marketing and BCS Card users have their own analytics page. You will receive an email with the link, and then another email with the encryption phrase.

    If you cannot find or have not received either email,  fill out a support ticket.
